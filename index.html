Industry

Major Fields with blockchain Technology

Finance

Blockchain for Finance

Blockchain could help reduce the overall transaction cost by eliminating any 3rd parties from the transaction chain. There will be no cost associated with third party, hence the costs to complete money transmittance across Blockchain would be cheaper than the current system.

Media

Blockchain for Media

Blockchain based solution and technology enables the users to create decentralized and secure systems. Due to decentralized nature, no single user can have complete control over the entire network and transactions are recorded in a streamlined and encrypted form called blocks.

Real Estate

Blockchain for Real Estate

One of the most complex challenges faced by the Real Estate Sector is Property appraisals, Mortgage, Title management, Deed/Lease transactions, Cash flow etc. The emergence of Blockchain promises the overhaul of current system and laying a strong foundation for next few decades.

Play

Blockchain for Play

Blockchain Fields also effect to video game field. Current Game system are based on centralized management and sometimes it makes users down to the risk. Blockchain makes this system based on decentralized ones and it will lead users to the new field of game method and trade.

Transportation

Blockchain for Transportation

The current system makes it difficult to track transactions between intermediaries between carrier and the consumer. The arrival of Blockchain technology is creating a ripple effect in Logistics and Transportation industry which is expected to grow $18 Trillion by the year 2025.

Government

Blockchain for Government

It is no more problem that check the fraud vote while blockchain technology is coming true. Voters will do it through chain transaction and Decentralized Smart Contract will check the transaction. This will guarantee the quality of vote and government system.
